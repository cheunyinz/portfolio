About me

Hello, my name is Cheun Yin Zhang. Currently I am in my final year of the study Communication Multimedia Design at the Hogeschool van Amsterdam. During this four year bachelor course I get to learn about the different aspects of digital design such as User Expierence Design, Visual Design, and Front-end development.

In the second year I chose to focus more on coding and programming. Here I got to furthermore improve myself with coding using HTML, CSS, and Javascript. On top of that I got the opportunity to learn the basics of back-end development by learning node.js and MongoDB.

During my 3-month internship at Social Brothers in my second year, I had the chance to apply everything I had learned in the real world. It was during this internship that I discovered my true passion for front-end development as a future career.
